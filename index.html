About

I am a Mechanical Engineering graduate student at the University of Pennsylvania, focused on robotics, mechatronics, and product design.

Before Penn, I worked at Atlas Copco as a Design Engineer, where I designed manufacturable mechanical components, supported custom compressor units, created engineering drawings, worked with PLM systems, and automated CAD workflows.

My goal is to build intelligent hardware products that combine strong mechanical design, embedded systems, rapid prototyping, and practical engineering judgment.
